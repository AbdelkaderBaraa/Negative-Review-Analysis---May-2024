# Negative-Review-Analysis---May-202

## 📋 Project Overview

This analysis examines negative guest reviews (ratings below 6) for two Copenhagen hostels:
- **Copenhagen Downtown Hostel (DWT)**
- **Nyhavn63 (BDW)**

The goal was to identify complaint patterns, understand guest sentiment, and pinpoint areas for improvement across multiple booking platforms ; Booking.com, HostelWorld, Airbnb, Expedia, Google Reviews, and Tripadvisor.

## 🔍 Key Findings

**Copenhagen Downtown Hostel:**
- Cleanliness is the main issue (36.6% of negative reviews)
- French guests are most critical (16.6% negative rate)
- Complaints increased significantly in May due to higher guest volume

**Nyhavn63:**
- Capsule maintenance is critical (poor locks, ventilation, cleanliness)
- Danish guests most dissatisfied (52.9% negative rate)
- Facilities and cleanliness are top concerns

---

## 💡 Main Recommendations

- Increase cleaning frequency and quality control
- Fix capsule locks and ventilation systems immediately
- Implement targeted service improvements for critical guest nationalities
- Establish preventive maintenance schedules
